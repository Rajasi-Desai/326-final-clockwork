Student Care Supply Closets

Everyone deserves uninhibited access to personal care supplies. The Dean of Students Office manages the Student Care Supply Closet which provide free toiletries and household items to students who are facing economic insecurity and/or an unexpected financial emergency. Any student who is in need is eligible to use this resourceâ€”no questions asked. Simply fill out this online shopping sheet and wait for a staff member to reach out to you to schedule your pick-up.

Items commonly available in the closets include:

  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Soap and body wash
  • Toothpaste and toothbrushes
  • Paper products
  • Cleaning products
  • Baby wipes and diapers
  • Pads and tampons
  • Hair products for natural hair
  • And more!

The Student Care Supply Closet team is also partnering with the Project Period UMass Amherst, a student organization to end period poverty by promoting menstrual equity and providing free menstrual products for everyone!
